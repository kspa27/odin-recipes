Curry

Description

Ingredients

    Rice
    Chicken
    Spices
    Crushed Tomatoes

Onions

  1. Cook rice in rice cooker
  2. Sear chicken and set aside.
  3. Add onions. Saute for 5 minutes then add spices. Saute for 30 Seconds
  4. Add Crushed Tomatoes and cook down
  5. Add chicken and reduce sauce for another 10 minutes
  6. Serve with rice (Will make this an actual recipe page later)
